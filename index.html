Speaking of the method behind the madness.

If you are viewing this web page on a device with a large screen you will notice the "z pattern". This article and the two above have purposely been placed into the "z pattern". This is a common UX website design practice and its purpose is to allow for quick and natural scanning of content. I use the term "scanning" because this is exactly what people do when viewing a website. People scan to find the content they want to read. Or they scan until they are presented with content the designer wants them to read.
